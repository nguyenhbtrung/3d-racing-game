Fantasy Skybox FREE - Release Notes

Version 1.6.5
• Compatible with Unity 2021.3.0 or higher.

• Rearranged the Scenes folder.

Version 1.6.0
• Compatible with Unity 2021.1.0 or higher.
• Added 32 cubemaps.
• Removed old demo scene.
• Added 2 demo scenes (with/without test terrain).

Version 1.5.6
• Added 16 low-quality panoramic skyboxes (FS013 and FS017).

Version 1.5.0
• Compatible with Unity 2019.3.0f1 or higher.
